Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade my plan?

Log into your dashboard and navigate to the billing section. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact support within 14 days of your purchase.

Is my data really private?

Absolutely. Zephyr stores your data on your own infrastructure. We do not have access to your files, period.
